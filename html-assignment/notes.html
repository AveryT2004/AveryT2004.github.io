HTML Tags and Their Meanings

"p"
Creates a paragraph on the site.
"h"
Creates a heading and changes the size depending on the
number afterwards, which can be 1 through 6.
"head"
The brain of the code, tells the website what
needs to be on it without showing it.
"br"
Ends the line of text and moves any remaining
text to the next line.
"body"
Tells the website what is actually being shown.
